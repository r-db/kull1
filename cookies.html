This Cookie Policy explains how KULL 1 uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and mobile application. By using the Platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit, such as your login status and preferences, which makes your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

We also use similar technologies including local storage, session storage, and web beacons that function similarly to cookies.

2. Cookies We Use

Category Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the Platform to function. Maintains your login session, security tokens, and tournament submission state. Cannot be disabled. Session / 30 days
Functional Remembers your preferences such as notification settings, dark/light mode, and tournament filter selections. 1 year
Analytics Helps us understand how users navigate the Platform so we can improve it. We use aggregated, anonymized data only. No individual tracking. 2 years
Payment (Stripe) Stripe places cookies to detect fraud and process payments securely. Governed by Stripe's own privacy and cookie policies. Varies

3. We Do Not Use Advertising Cookies

KULL 1 does not place advertising or behavioral tracking cookies. We do not use retargeting pixels, ad networks, or third-party cookies for marketing purposes. What happens on the Platform stays on the Platform.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using core Platform features.

Browser-specific instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data

5. Mobile App

Our mobile application uses equivalent persistent storage (such as AsyncStorage on React Native) to maintain session state and preferences. These function identically to cookies and are governed by this same policy. You can clear app data through your device's application settings.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add new features or change our technology stack. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance.

Questions about cookies or privacy? Contact us at privacy@kull1.com or use our contact form.
