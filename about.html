About

The Mission

Built For The
Water Tribe

KULL 1 was built by kayak anglers who were tired of paper weight slips, honor-system scoring, and tournament results nobody could verify a week later. We wanted a platform that treated competitive kayak fishing like the legitimate sport it is — with live leaderboards, GPS-verified catches, and a permanent record that follows an angler for their entire career.

The name comes from the first qualifier. The gate you have to get past before the real competition begins. Not everyone makes it. The ones who do know what it took to get there.

Today KULL 1 powers clubs and tournaments across the country, from single-lake club tournaments to multi-state circuits. We handle the technology so Tournament Directors can focus on running great events, and anglers can focus on fishing.

How We're Different

No Disputes.
No Gray Areas.

01

GPS Verification

Every catch submission includes a GPS coordinate at time of submission. No docks. No parking lots. If the pin doesn't match the water, the catch doesn't count.

02

Photo + Length

Anglers submit a photo of the fish on a measuring board with length recorded to the hundredth of an inch. The AI flags obvious issues. Directors have final say.

03

Live Leaderboards

Results update in real time as catches come in. Anglers, family, and sponsors can watch the tournament unfold from anywhere. No waiting until weigh-in.

04

Permanent Record

Every catch, every tournament, every placement — preserved permanently in the angler's profile. Your career on the water, documented for as long as you fish.

05

Director Tools

Create events, set rules, manage registrations, review catch submissions, and run payouts — all from a single dashboard. No spreadsheets. No phone tag.

06

Fair Payouts

Entry fees are collected and held by KULL 1. Payouts go directly to anglers after the tournament closes. No director float. No late checks. No excuses.
