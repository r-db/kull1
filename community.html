What's a Club

Your Circuit Home Base.

A KULL 1 Club is where your tournament circuit lives. Created by a Tournament Director, it holds everything your angler base needs — event schedules, live leaderboards, Angler of the Year standings, and a growing record of every verified catch your circuit has produced.

—

Tournament Schedule — all upcoming events, entry fees, and registration links in one place

—

Angler of the Year — cumulative points across every event your club runs

—

Verified Results — every past tournament result, GPS-locked and permanent

—

Angler Profiles — every angler who has competed in your circuit, with their verified record

—

Shareable Link — one URL to your entire club page, ready to share to your existing Facebook group, email list, or anywhere else

What We're Building

Real Anglers.
Verified Catches.
Shared Waters.

01

Shared Waters

KULL 1 clubs form around specific bodies of water and regional circuits. The anglers in your club know the same coves, the same seasonal patterns, the same ramps. That shared knowledge is the foundation of a real fishing community.

02

Verified Competition

When results are verified, the standings mean something. The number one angler in your circuit earned that position on the water — GPS-locked catch by catch. No disputes. No doubt about who's actually the best on your home lake.

03

Career Records

Your KULL 1 record follows you. The catch you made at age 22 on Lake Lanier is still there when you're 45 fishing a different circuit in a different state. A permanent verified fishing record has never existed before KULL 1.

04

Community Growth

The more anglers who join your circuit's club, the deeper the standings become. Clubs grow when the infrastructure is trustworthy. KULL 1's verification creates the conditions for circuits to grow past the Facebook group and into a real league.
