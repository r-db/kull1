How It Works

Three Steps.
Zero Gray Area.

01

Find Your Tournament

Browse open events in your area. See the entry fee, the prize structure, and the circuit standings before you register. Pay entry directly in the app — no Venmo, no cash at the ramp.

02

Submit Your Catch

Photo, species, weight, and length — the app locks your GPS coordinates and timestamp at the moment you hit submit. Before the fish goes back. The record is permanent from that second forward.

03

Watch Your Rank Move

Standings update in real time as anglers submit across the circuit. Your current position, the weight you need to move, and the full leaderboard — visible throughout the tournament day.

Verification

Why GPS Timestamping Changes Everything

01

Locked at Submission

The timestamp and GPS coordinates are captured at the moment you tap submit — not when you later upload, not when you get cell service back. The moment of submission is the record.

02

Photo + Data Together

Your photo, weight, species, length, GPS, and timestamp are bundled as a single verified submission. One package. Nothing can be edited after the fact.

03

Disputed Catches Don't Exist

If the data says the catch was made at 11:42 AM on Lake Seminole with a 4.2 lb largemouth, that is the official record. No argument possible. The hardware doesn't negotiate.

04

Your Data, Forever

Every verified catch you've ever made in a KULL 1 event lives in your permanent record. Tournament circuits can come and go — your record doesn't.
